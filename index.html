1. Information We Collect

We collect information to operate and improve the Service. The types of information include:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Grocery expense entries: item name, category, quantity/units, unit price, total spend, store/market name, purchase date, notes, and optional photos/receipts you upload.
  • Budgets & lists: monthly/weekly budgets, shopping lists, and tags you create.
  • Account information (if you create an account): name or username, email address, and password (stored with industry-standard hashing).
  • Support messages: content you send us for help or feedback.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & app data: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, performance metrics, crash logs, and usage analytics (e.g., screens viewed, feature interactions).
  • Approximate location (optional): if you enable location tagging for stores, we may process approximate location to assist with store suggestions. You can disable location at any time in device settings.
  • Local storage: some preferences and cached data may be stored on your device for offline use and faster performance.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Analytics and crash reporting services: aggregated or pseudonymized insights to improve stability and user experience.
  • Cloud services: if you opt to sync or back up data, your entries may be stored with our cloud provider under contractual safeguards.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the Service, including saving entries, budgets, and generating summaries and trends.
  • Personalize features (e.g., quick-add items, recent stores, budget reminders).
  • Monitor performance, fix bugs, and enhance security.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
  • Optional: with your consent where required, send product tips or newsletters (you can opt out anytime).

3. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

If you are in the EEA or UK, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to deliver the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain and improve the Service, secure it, prevent abuse, and understand usage, balanced against your rights.
  • Consent: for optional features such as precise location or marketing communications. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with applicable laws.

4. How We Share Information

  • Service providers: vetted vendors (e.g., analytics, hosting, crash reporting) under confidentiality and data protection commitments.
  • Legal and safety: when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety of users or others.
  • Business transfers: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, information may be transferred subject to this Policy or a successor policy with notice where required.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not “share” it for cross-context behavioral advertising as defined by U.S. state privacy laws.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may delete your account or specific entries in-app (where available) or by contacting us. Deletion requests will be completed subject to legal requirements and reasonable backup cycles.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures (e.g., encryption in transit, hashed passwords) to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Right to know/access categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to deletion, subject to exceptions.

  • Right to opt out of sales or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising (not applicable as we do not sell/share as defined).

You or your authorized agent can exercise rights by emailing Glemadmail@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

7.2 EEA/UK

  • Right to restrict or object to processing based on legitimate interests.

7.3 Managing Choices

  • Location: disable location permissions to stop location processing.
  • Marketing emails: use the unsubscribe link or contact us.
  • Account/data deletion: use in-app controls (if available) or email us.
  • Sync/backups: you can turn cloud sync off at any time (if offered).

8. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.
